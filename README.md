Project Overview

This repository contains an end-to-end exploratory data analysis (EDA) and reporting project for Vrinda Store. The goal is to analyze 2022 sales data to understand customer behaviour, identify top-performing categories, regions and channels, and provide data-driven recommendations to grow sales in 2023.

Business Objective

Create an annual sales report for 2022 that answers key business questions and provides actionable recommendations to help Vrinda target the most valuable customer segments and channels.

Key Questions Addressed

Compare monthly sales vs. orders in a single chart.
Which month had the highest sales and orders?
Which gender purchased more (men vs women) in 2022?
What were the different order statuses in 2022?
Top 10 states contributing to sales.
Relationship between age groups and gender.
Which sales channels contributed most to revenue?
Which product categories sold the most?

Main Findings (summary)

Women accounted for ~65% of purchases.
Top states by sales: Maharashtra, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh (~35% combined).
Top age group: Adults aged 30–49 (~50% contribution).
Top channels: Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra (~80% combined).
Recommendation: Focus marketing and promotions on women aged 30–49 in Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh via campaigns and special offers on Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra.
